- Goal: Replace those duplicated literal uses with a single controller-scoped constant named `OBO_ID_FIELD` without changing the public contract or internal runtime semantics.
- In scope: Add `OBO_ID_FIELD` as a private class-level constant and replace the exact standalone `obo_id` occurrences described by Sonar in `V1SearchController`.
- Out of scope: changing request mappings, renaming public fields or parameters, or merging this change with unrelated refactors, including reverting or reformatting already-committed constant extractions such as `SHORT_FORM_FIELD`.
