- Goal: Keep the local handler and add an explanatory nested comment without changing 404 behavior.
- In scope:
  - Update only `handleError(HttpServletRequest req, Exception exception)` in `backend/src/main/java/uk/ac/ebi/spot/ols/controller/api/v1/V1OntologyPropertyController.java`.

  - Keep the change local to this controller.

  - Touching other controllers with similar handlers.
